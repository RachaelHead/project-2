The History of Acupuncture

Acupuncture is practice that originates from Traditional Chinese Medicine. In acupuncture practice, the body has an energy, Qi (pronounced chee), that flows through meridians and pathways. Along the pathways, the body has more than 2,000 acupuncture points that practitioners puncture with needles to relieve pain and improve disease.
